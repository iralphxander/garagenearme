Q: How often should I get my exotic car serviced? A: It depends on the brand and usage-but typically, every 10,000 km or once a year for routine service, plus immediate checks if any warning lights or unusual behaviour appear.

Q: Does pickup service cost extra? A: Some garages include it as part of their luxury service package; others might charge a small premium. Always ask upfront.

Q: Will using an independent specialised garage void my warranty? A: For out‑of‑warranty vehicles, independent specialist garages can offer excellent service. Q: What kind of parts should I expect? A: Genuine OEM parts or manufacturer‑approved equivalents. Look for transparency about parts sourcing and quality.

Q: Can they handle tuning and performance upgrades? A: Yes-top luxury car garages often provide performance tuning, software upgrades, and customisation alongside standard servicing.
